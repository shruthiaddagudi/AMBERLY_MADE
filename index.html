Our Story

Amberly Made
by Shruthi & Deepthi

What began as two friends sharing creativity, colors, and countless little moments slowly blossomed into something more meaningful.

Between conversations, laughter, late-night ideas, and patient hands at work, Amberly Made was born — a space where simple materials are transformed into pieces filled with warmth, intention, and love.

Every creation tells a story. A story of friendship, imagination, and the joy of making something by hand in a world that often moves too fast. We believe the most beautiful things aren’t rushed; they’re crafted thoughtfully, cherished deeply, and made with heart.

Amberly Made is more than a collection of handmade pieces. It’s a reflection of who we are, what we love, and the happiness we hope to share with others.

Thank you for being a part of our journey and for celebrating the beauty of handmade creations with us. 🤍

Made slowly. Made thoughtfully. Made with love.

Shruthi & Deepthi ✦
